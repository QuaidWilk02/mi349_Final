Characters

College Masters will have many characters you can befriend and possibly get closer to. These potential suitors or friends include, but not limited to: an odd student you pass on your commute to school, the student that sits in front of you, your co-worker, your neighbor, and more possible options. Plus, you have a cat to pet when life gets a little too stressful.

College Masters is a visual novel/dating sim. Form relationships and bonds by talking to people and making decisions that can affect how different people see you.
